# Meet Agent Penny

**Your personal AI assistant for turning everyday information into clear next steps.**

Penny helps you understand your day, make sense of messages, remember useful context, research the web, draft polished text, and work with connected tools when they are enabled.

> Ask for a brief, a draft, a plan, a search, a summary, or a workflow. Penny can meet you where you are.

---

## ✨ What Penny Can Help With

| Area | Penny can |
| --- | --- |
| 📝 **Daily planning** | Build a practical brief, review commitments, and surface what matters next. |
| 📅 **Calendar** | Read calendars, summarize events, and create or update events when Google Calendar is connected. |
| ✉️ **Mail** | Summarize recent email, find important threads, and prepare Gmail drafts for review. |
| 🌐 **Research** | Search or fetch web pages when web tools are configured, then turn findings into concise answers. |
| 🧠 **Memory** | Remember stable preferences and useful details across conversations. |
| 🛠️ **Skills** | Learn custom instructions and workflows you teach it, so specialized tasks happen your way. |
| 🎨 **Creative work** | Brainstorm, write, edit, explain, and shape rough ideas into finished material. |
| 🎙️ **Voice** | Listen and respond by voice when audio input and speech output are enabled. |

---

## 🚀 Good Ways To Start

| Try asking | Useful when you want |
| --- | --- |
| **"Create a daily brief."** | A grounded snapshot of your day. |
| **"What's on my calendar today?"** | A quick schedule check. |
| **"Summarize the latest 10 emails."** | The signal without the inbox dive. |
| **"Draft a reply to this message."** | A polished response you can review. |
| **"Research this topic and give me the tradeoffs."** | Help deciding what matters. |
| **"Remember that I prefer concise summaries with clear next steps."** | Penny to adapt across future chats. |

---

## 🧩 Make Penny Yours

Penny is your personal assistant, so shape the conversation around how you like to work.

| Customize | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| 🎚️ **Tone** | Concise, warm, direct, formal, playful, or anything in between. |
| 📋 **Format** | Checklists, tables, summaries, drafts, plans, or step-by-step notes. |
| 🔎 **Style** | Quick answers, deeper analysis, creative brainstorming, or practical next actions. |
| 🧠 **Memory** | Preferences and stable details you want Penny to remember for future chats. |
| 🛠️ **Skills** | Custom instructions, workflows, and reusable task patterns you teach Penny. |

---

**You stay in control.** When a tool might change your data, such as creating a calendar event or updating an email draft, Penny will ask for confirmation first.
